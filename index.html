Long live all the magic we made

I've always liked creating and building things. Software became a way for me to explore what I'm curious about and turn those ideas into something useful for other people.

I am drawn to difficult engineering problems, elegant abstractions, and user experiences that feel clear, fluid, and deeply considered. What interests me is not just the technology itself, but the world around it: the context we build in, the constraints we inherit, and the possibilities that open up when systems are shaped with care.

I'm the founder of Structured Labs and one of the makers of Waldium. I want to build technology that is rigorous at its core and generous in the way it meets people, technology that brings clarity to complexity and creates room for new ideas, new forms of work, and new ways of seeing.
